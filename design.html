DESIGN & ART

I made this die-cut of a packaging containing perfume, with the logo of a fox, for a competition I participated in 2022 and win.

"Montessori Street" is a project devised by the I.I.S. Podesti Calzecchi Onesti in collaboration with the municipality of Chiaravalle, for which various panels were created in 2021 to promote schools that have adopted the Montessori method.

The first project is a packaging made for Alma, a phytocosmetic company. The second project is an entrance ticket to the exhibition "Eros & Thanatos - beauty and its abyss". Finally, the third project serves to sponsor a festival dedicated to the 33rd anniversary of Photoshop.

In this project I had to create a butterfly in a painterly and geometric style.
